Generative AI has changed the world of design forever. But the secret to getting great results isn't the AI itself‚Äîit's the prompt. Here are tested prompts for Midjourney v6 and newer.

1. Cinematic Realism

To get that movie-like quality, focus on lighting, camera type, and film stock.

  • /imagine prompt: A futuristic cyberpunk street food vendor, neon lights, rain-slicked streets, cinematic lighting, shot on 35mm lens, f/1.8, bokeh effect, hyper-realistic, --ar 16:9 --v 6.0
  • /imagine prompt: Portrait of an elderly astronaut looking out a spaceship window, reflection of Earth in visor, soft dramatic lighting, highly detailed texture, --ar 3:4
üí° Pro Tip:
Use negative prompts (--no) to remove unwanted elements. Example: --no blur, cartoon, illustration

2. Minimalist Vector Style

Perfect for websites, app icons, and branding.

  • /imagine prompt: simple vector logo of a fox, flat design, white background, minimal, geometric shapes, vibrant orange color --no shadow, gradient
  • /imagine prompt: isometric illustration of a cozy home office, pastel colors, 3d render style but flat shading, clean lines --ar 4:3

3. Artistic Styles

Mimic famous art styles or specific mediums.

  • /imagine prompt: A stormy ocean landscape in the style of Vincent Van Gogh, impasto texture, swirling clouds, oil painting
  • /imagine prompt: A cute robot watering plants, watercolor painting, wet-on-wet technique, soft edges, pastel palette --ar 1:1

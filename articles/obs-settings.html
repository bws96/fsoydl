Best OBS Settings for Low-End PCs

By Tech Support ‚Ä¢ Updated Jan 21, 2026

Streaming on a budget laptop? You don't need a $3,000 rig to start. Adjusting a few hidden settings in OBS Studio can double your FPS.

1. Output Settings

Go to Settings > Output. Set your "Encoder" to Hardware (NVENC) if you have an NVIDIA card. If not, use x264 but set the "CPU Usage Preset" to ultrafast.

üí° Pro Tip:
Lower your "Output (Scaled) Resolution" to 720p (1280x720). It looks fine on mobile screens and saves huge processing power.

2. Video Bitrate

Don't set this too high. For 720p streaming, 2500 Kbps to 3500 Kbps is the sweet spot.
